How to Build a Digital Portfolio

Because we are all design majors, we felt it would be useful to do some research about creating a portfolio. Having a strong portfolio is incredibly important for designers as that is an employer's first impression on your work, so we need to know what to put into a portfolio and how to make it look nice. Since this is so relevant to all of us, we decided to go for this as our final topic.

Suggested Websites

Out of all the Portfolios we reviewed, there were 4 host sites that were the most common:

  • Wix
  • SquareSpace
  • WordPress
  • Readymag
  • and Pixpa

    • We suggest that you look into these websites when you begin looking for a platform to host your digital portfolio!
